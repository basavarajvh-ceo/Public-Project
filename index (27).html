Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Choicesphere (OPC) Private Limited

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, mobile number, email address, pincode, and any information you voluntarily submit as part of project proposals or account registration. We also collect usage data to improve platform performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Public Project platform — to verify identity, facilitate project proposals, connect contractors and investors, and enable transparent monitoring of infrastructure projects.

3. Public Transparency

Project proposals, statuses, funding outcomes, and milestone updates are publicly visible. This is a core feature of the platform — radical transparency is our political shield against corruption. Personal contact details are never made public.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only shared internally between platform stakeholders (admin, contractor, investor) as required to complete a project.

5. SMS & OTP

OTP is sent via 2Factor.in for identity verification only. Your mobile number is not used for unsolicited marketing.

6. Data Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) with industry-standard security. We use HTTPS for all communication. Admin access requires OTP + password authentication.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion or correction of your personal data by contacting us. We will respond within 7 working days.
