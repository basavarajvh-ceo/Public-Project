Privacy Policy
Choicesphere OPC Pvt Ltd

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Choicesphere (OPC) Private Limited

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, mobile number, email address (if provided), pincode, district, and any information you voluntarily submit as part of project proposals or account registration. We also collect usage data to improve platform performance.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate the Public Project platform — to verify your identity, facilitate project proposals, connect contractors and investors, and enable transparent monitoring of infrastructure projects.

3. Public Transparency

Project proposals, statuses, funding outcomes, and milestone updates are publicly visible. This is a core feature of the platform — radical transparency is our political shield against corruption. Your personal contact details are never made public.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is only shared internally between platform stakeholders (admin, contractor, investor) as required to process a project.

5. SMS & OTP

OTP is sent via 2Factor.in for identity verification only. Your mobile number is never used for unsolicited marketing or shared with advertisers.

6. Data Security

All data is stored on Google Firebase (Firestore) with industry-standard security. We use HTTPS for all communication. Admin access requires OTP + password authentication.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion or correction of your personal data by contacting us. We will respond within 7 working days.

8. Children's Privacy

This platform is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Cookies & Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your language preference and session data locally on your device. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Terms & Conditions

Effective: April 2026 · Choicesphere (OPC) Private Limited · CIN: U62013KA2026OPC218620

1. About the Platform

Public Project is a private B2B SaaS civic-tech platform operated by Choicesphere (OPC) Private Limited. It connects citizens, contractors, and CSR investors for rural infrastructure transparency. It is not a government portal and is not affiliated with any government body.

2. Platform Approval

All proposals, contractors, and investors are approved by platform admins only — not any government body. Admin decisions are final for platform listing purposes.

3. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and truthful information at all times
  • Do not submit fraudulent or duplicate proposals
  • Do not misuse the platform for political campaigning
  • One account per mobile number
  • Misuse will result in permanent account ban

4. Citizen Rights

As a citizen, you have the right to raise infrastructure proposals, track all project statuses publicly, support (vote for) any proposal, raise complaints about projects, and download public project data.

5. Content Policy

Submitted proposals become part of the public platform record. Platform admins reserve the right to reject proposals that are incomplete, duplicate, outside scope, or politically motivated.

6. Payments & Fees

The platform is currently free for citizens during the launch phase. Any future fees will be communicated transparently in advance.

7. Transparency Commitment

Project statuses, funding amounts, contractor details, and milestones are publicly visible. Complaints are publicly logged. This radical transparency is the platform's core promise and cannot be disabled.

8. Liability

Choicesphere (OPC) Private Limited facilitates connections between stakeholders but does not guarantee project completion, funding outcomes, or government approvals. The platform serves as a transparency and accountability tool.

9. Governing Law

These terms are governed by the laws of India. Any disputes shall be subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
